Dashboard Group 3 – HVAC

The HVAC dashboard group presents information relating to HVAC services, including:

  • Space Heating / Heating Services
  • Space Cooling / Cooling Services
  • Packaged AC
  • Critical Cooling
  • Domestic Hot / Cold Water
  • Ventilation

Overview

The HVAC overview page includes:

  • Top horizontal cards with active device counts
  • Building thermal performance chart with recent historical actual heating & cooling outputs vs estimated seasonal demand
  • Live and compensated weather demand

Building thermal performance - Target characteristics:

  • Cooling mode in summer with minimal heating
  • Heating mode in winter with minimal cooling
  • Reduced heating and cooling output outside of summer/winter seasons
  • Minimal dual heating and cooling
  • Heating and cooling outputs do not exceed weather demands
  • Heating and cooling output phasing correlate with weather demands

Tenants

Clicking 'Tenants' navigates to a list of building tenants or areas, showing live HVAC status. Selecting a tenant shows its equipment list

Clicking an equipment item shows its recent historical performance profile:

Tenant HVAC equipment item - Target characteristics:

  • Temperature line should follow seasonal expectations (hovering above setpoint in summer, below in winter)
  • Heating/cooling outputs should not overlap
  • Minimal heating in summer, minimal cooling in winter
  • Enable indicator should align with zone time schedule

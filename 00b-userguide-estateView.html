Estate Overview

    Dashboard contains (where data is available) :

  • Top row cards with high level estate/platform status metrics
  • Tabulated list of connected buildings, with live energy & water demands, and active FDD flag count
  • Live energy distribution pie chart
  • Actual recent energy demand per building (combined gas energy + electricity)
  • Relative recent energy demand per building (total energy per 1K square meters)

Utility Analysis

Clicking to drill down on a specific primary service navigates to a report for the selected service:

Target report characteristics:

  • Month comparisons should be less than or equal to previous
  • Day, week, month usages should align to targets
  • Usage profiles should correlate with actual building usage pattern

Further drilldown to tenant and landlord usage of the selected primary service is possible with the 'Landlord' and 'Tenants' buttons at the lower left of the page

Dashboard Group 7 – Health

Overview

The Health dashboard group presents information from Attain’s lifecycle management system. The overview page contains a tabulated list of all connected equipment:

[Image: Equipment Health Overview Table]

Equipment health is broken down into physical health, serviceability, and energy health. Intelligent maintenance routines can be initiated through Attain, with any work statuses appearing in the Maintenance Activity column.

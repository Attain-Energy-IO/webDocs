The primary services dashboard group presents utility consumption information

2.0 - Overview

    Dashboard contains :

  • 30 days building electricity, gas & water demand

    Target characteristics :

  • Building has data populated - Data should be made available from EMS, BMS, or billing provider
  • Clear correlation between day-of-week profiles

2.1 - Utility drilldown

Clicking on ANALYSIS button at top of utility consumption chart navigates to a utility usage report :

    Dashboard contains :

  • Previous month consumption compared with same month previoius year
  • Current month consumption compared with same month previoius year
  • Yesterday (24 hour) consumption
  • Last week (7 day) consumption
  • Various aggregations on yesterday and last week charts
  • Day, Week, Month target consumptions (calculaed from benchmark)
  • Buttons to further breakdown utility consumption accross areas / tenants & landlord

    Target characteristics :

  • Green bars (this year) lower than utility coloured bars (last year)
  • Day Total (top right hand chart) near to or less than Day Target (bottom row right hand card)
  • Week Total (bottom chart) near to or less than Week Target (bottom row)
  • Month Total (current month bar chart, green bar) near to or less than Month Target (bottom row)

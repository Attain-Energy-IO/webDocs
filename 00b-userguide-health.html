The equipment health dashboard group presents equipment maintenance and lifecycle management info

6.0 - Overview

    Dashboard contains :

  • Tabulated equipment health information
  • Physical health indication progress bar (green = new / red = end of life) as a function of age and usage
  • Servicability indication progress bar (green = fully servicable / red = out of service) driven from equipment fault status
  • Energy health indication progress bar (green = good / red = poor) driven from energy related FDD statuses
  • Indication of last service time, and status of any smart maintenance workflows being driven through platform logic

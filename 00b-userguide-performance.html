Dashboard Group 1 – Performance

The performance dashboard group presents energy performance of the building and each tenant or area within the building.

1.0 - Overview

    Dashboard contains :

  • Target energy usage intensity in kWh/sqM/year - Generally set from customer defined benchmark
  • Actual energy usage intensity in kWh/sqM/year - Rolling previous 12 month
  • Total energy saving in last 12 months and 12 months previous - Calculated from baseline average
  • Charts with total monthly primary electrical and gas energy consumption for the previous 3 years
  • Tabulated tenant/area 12-month running energy performance - Click row for popup chart with previous 3 years energy consumption

    Target characteristics :

  • Building has data populated - Attain can work with data from any source
  • Actual EUI is near to Target EUI (dashboard top, left hand, two kWh/sqM/year values)
  • Positive energy saving metrics for previous two years
  • Tenant running EUI has light colour background, indicating close to target benchmark

1.1.1 - Electrical Performance

Clicking on ANALYSIS button at top of Electrical Consumption chart navigates to an electrical service performance page :

Dashboard contains:

  • Grouped electrical services 30 day actual consumption VS target consumption (from benchmark or NABERS)
  • Tabulated tenant/area 30 day average electrical power VS target electrical power & other energy metrics

Target characteristics:

  • Actual total electricity consumption per system - Metering system must support system-by-system measurement
  • Tenant average month power should be less than or equal to target
  • 12 Month EUI should not be coloured cell - Gray means over benchmark

1.2.1 - Electrical Performance - Tenant Drilldown

Clicking a Floors Electrical Usage table row navigates to an electrical service performance page for the selected tenant

1.1.2 - Gas Energy Performance

If gas service exists in building, clicking on ANALYSIS button at top of Gas Energy Consumption chart navigates to a gas service performance page :

Dashboard contains:

  • Main system usage should be less than or equal to system benchmark
  • Tenant average month power should be less than or equal to target
  • 12 Month EUI should not be coloured cell - Gray means over benchmark

Target characteristics:

  • Main system usage should be less than or equal to system benchmark
  • Tenant average month power should be less than or equal to target
  • 12 Month EUI should not be coloured cell - Gray means over benchmark

1.2.2 - Gas Energy Performance - Tenant Drilldown

Generally applicable to residential or multi unit sites with extensive gas distribution, clicking a Secondary Gas Usage table row navigates to a gas service performance page for the selected consumer

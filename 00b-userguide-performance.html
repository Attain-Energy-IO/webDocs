Dashboard Group 1 – Performance

The performance dashboard group presents energy performance of the building and each tenant or area within the building.

Overview

  • Charts with total monthly primary electrical and gas energy consumption for the previous 3 years
  • Active overall energy benchmark information
  • Tabulated tenant/area 12-month running energy performance
